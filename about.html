About our restuarant

Grab and Bite is a small restuarant with a very well prepared

dishes. Our staff is well trained, specailly those who work in the kitchen.

We are situated in durban.The restaurant was established on the 29th of September 2015.

By owner Sindiswa Dlamini and Inamandla Luthuli. The restaurant

operates 10 hours a day, 5 days a week, from 10 am to 8pm.
